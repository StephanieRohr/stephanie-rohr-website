Stephanie first discovered her passion for video during the beginning of YouTube's fruition. She began making videos with her friends to post on Facebook and created the series "The Musical Morons" (a 2-part video series) when she was around 12 years old. She grew up pursuing many different art forms, including art, music, dance, colorguard, and musical theater. Later on, when enrolled in college, she decided to pursue electronic media production, as she felt it encapsulated all of those art forms into one perfect medium. <br /><br />

Stephanie Rohr is a graduate from High Point University with a Bachelor of Arts degree in Electronic Media Production and minor in Music. She also completed her Associate's in Applied Sciences in Entertainment Technologies: Recording Engineering at Guilford Technical Community College. She currently works as a Video Editor for Served With Honor, LLC.
